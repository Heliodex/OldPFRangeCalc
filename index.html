FAQ:

Q: Why does it say "Somebody has left" instead of their username?
A: The "user has left" code is currently under maintinence, and it will be fixed soon.

Q: How do I become a moderator or an admin?
A: We are currently not offering any more spaces on our moderation or administrator team.

Q: Why can't we have spaces in our usernames?
A: Spaces in usernames confuse some people, and make it more difficult to filter certain words.

DocSocial's Privacy Policy


When you input a message into DocSocial, it is sent out only to any current members of the chatroom. Messages are NEVER stored on our servers.
Keep in mind that members of the chatroom are allowed to screenshot and copy any data that you send, but only in the ways outlined in the End User Licence Agreement.
Usernames are only visible to others while you are in the chatroom, and after you leave, any new people visiting DocSocial afterwards will be unable to see your username.

Under GDPR Laws, you remain in control of your data. Please refer to the respective privacy policies of Netlify and Scaledrone to see what they do with messages.


Please check this privacy policy regularly. We may update it at any time without notice. You must agree to any changes if you continue to use DocSocial after the privacy policy has been updated.

This privacy policy was last updated 9th June 2021.
